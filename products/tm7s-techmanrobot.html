TM7S

  • TM7S is yet another regular payload cobot that is efficient and designed to reduce the production cycle time. It is suited for a variety of applications such as 3D bin picking, assembly, labeling, pick and place, PCB handling, polishing and deburring, quality inspection, screw driving and more. It is a reliable cobot that carries out tasks with accuracy and precision.
