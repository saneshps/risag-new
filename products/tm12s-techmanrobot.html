TM12S

  • A medium payload cobot from the TM AI series, the TM12S is designed to reduce production cycle time, resulting in a more faster and efficient operation, rendering quality output.

    It is used for a variety of applications such as conveyor tracking, machine tending, welding, packaging, PCB handling, palletizing, polishing , deburring and more.

Additional Information

Movement detection and Recording

This upcoming feature of the cobot will allow the cobots to track and record the human movements. This motion data can later be edited using the motion editor program.

Safety certifications

Our cobot has secured the domestic, international safety and cleanroom certification of the highest order (ISO Class 2) among the line of collaborative robots.

Two dimensional marker tracking

The precise alignment of the robotic arm is achieved through 2D vision and markers.

Robot Visual Safety

The dual 3D camera and built-in safety system of the cobots ensure 360° protection, doing away with the need for additional physical fences or barriers. It is also possible to incorporate other safety protocols as well.
