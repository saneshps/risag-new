AI Mobile Cobot

  • This is an innovative combination of cobot arms with AGVs or AMRs resulting in a mobile cobot that has multi-function capability, flexibility and mobility. With built-in AI and vision technology, it becomes a more smarter and efficient system.

    Features
    • Cobots use a DC power system, hence they can be easily connected with most of the AGV and AMR brands.
    • These cobots meet the SEMI-S2 safety standard, which is essential and advantageous if they are being used in the semiconductor industry.
    • Has the same advanced AI and built-in vision system as the stationary cobot series.

    Express Interest For the Product

Flexible and Compatible

Our mobile cobots utilize the TM Landmark function that helps them to understand where they are in a given 3D space and also recognize the objects around it.

Quickly Adjust The Cobot Movements

Our cobots feature a graphical interface that helps the operators to easily set up the task. It takes much less time to program the cobot, even if it is working on multiple locations in the factory.

Safe Working Environment

The TM cobot’s safety features can be further enhanced by installing LiDAR or safety sensors. You can set up low-speed areas or deactivate areas for the cobots that allows the cobot to slow down or halt its movement respectively.
