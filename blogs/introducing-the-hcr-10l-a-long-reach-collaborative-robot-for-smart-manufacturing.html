Introducing the HCR-10L: A Long-Reach Collaborative Robot for Smart Manufacturing

  • Conclusion

Having entered the fifth industrial revolution, intelligent automation has reached a new level. The well anticipated human-robot collaboration is now made possible with the advent of collaborative robots or cobots. Cobots work hand-in-hand with humans, handling repetitive tasks, allowing human workers to give their total attention to works that require more focus.

The new HCR series of Hanwha Robotics presents the HCR-10L . This advanced collaborative robot works safely alongside human operators, effectively carrying out tasks such as palletizing, loading, machine tending, and also works that require painting and welding.

This blog briefly introduces HCR-10L and its characteristics.

HCR-10L: Smart Collaborator

The new member of the HCR line up, HCR-10L features the longest arm reach in its class. It is equipped with advanced AI powered 3D vision that aids in the detection of random objects, identification of exact pick up points and insertion of parts into machine tools.

Key features:

  • Longest Arm Reach

    • HCR-10L has an impressive reach of 1800mm, which allows it to work efficiently in hard-to-reach areas. This cobot assists in the precise welding and surface painting by accurately positioning the tools.

  • High Payload

    • Coming with a payload of 10kg, HCR-10L is well-suited for moving heavier parts. It can also be used to mount painting or welding equipment within this weight range.

  • Painting Application

    • HCR-10L is most suited for painting applications due to its smooth and precise movements. It renders the even application of paint layers and can easily cover large areas and hard-to-reach places with its flexible arm.

  • Precise Welding

    • HCR-10L is built sturdy and equipped with advanced motion control, making it an ideal choice for welding tasks. It offers stable and accurate positioning of the tools for precise welds, especially on the larger components and complex structures that demand varied welding angles.

  • Safety Features

    • The HCR-10L robots feature AI collision detection and the option for instant halt. This allows for the safe collaboration of workers and cobots, especially during the painting and welding tasks, doing away with the need for safety barriers.

    In addition, these cobots have secured the domestic and international safety and clean room certification of the highest order, ISO Class 2, among the line of collaborative robots.

Conclusion

If you are looking for a durable and reliable collaboration of cobots and humans, then HCR-10L is the ideal choice. Its robust design and advanced AI prowess effectively automate the production process while maintaining strict safety standards.
