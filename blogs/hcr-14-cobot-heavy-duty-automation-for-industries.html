Applications - HCR - 14

Listed below are the major applications of HCR-14 cobots.

Palletizing & Depalletizing

As you are aware now of its extended reach and loading capacity which makes it the best partner for palletizing activities where you need to stack and organize the bulky boxes or components on the pallets. These cobots are also highly helpful in depalletizing which helps the job to be completed in limited time and with less stress and strain on the operator.

Machine Tending

HCR - 14 cobots are capable of operating CNC machines, injection molding machines as well as pressbrakes by helping to load and unload components or materials. The repeatability feature will make sure consistency in handling and their collaborative nature will permit integration safely along with human operators.

Material Handling

Be it the transfer of heavy materials between workstations or sorting and rearrangement of products, HCR-14 is made to take care of physically straining material handling activities.

Welding & Assembly

In intensive assembly lines, HCR-14 series cobots are capable of performing spot welding at extreme accuracy, part replacements or addition as well as torque based screwdriving. Its huge loading capacity also helps in supporting huge tools and end-effectors.
