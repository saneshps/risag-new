Training & Service

Training

We provide basic training programs for individuals and companies who want to learn collaborative robot (cobot) operation. Our training includes hands-on experience with real cobot systems, covering setup, programming, safety, and basic applications to help users quickly understand and start working with cobots.

Service

We offer reliable after-sales service and technical support to ensure your cobot systems operate smoothly. Our service team assists with installation, troubleshooting, maintenance, and technical guidance whenever needed.
