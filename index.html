Cobots: Reliable, Safe, Convenient

RAIV(Robot AI Vision)

The intelligent vision-based features enhance the TMflow Vision Node with advanced capabilities such as barcode reading, positioning, visual inspection and dimension measurement, thus increasing the production quality and operational efficiency.

Motion tracking and Recording

The cobot combines built-in vision and force sensing to ensure precise movement and controlled operation. It accurately positions itself while adjusting pressure in real time, making tasks like deburring and polishing easier.

System Accessories

A range of accessories compatible with the cobot set up is available, including camera mounting solutions, IO extension cables, calibration tools and many more.

Safety Certifications

The Cobots are certified to major international safety standards which includes the SGS-certified compliance for UL/CSA and CE. This ensures the safe human and robot collaboration ensuring high productivity.

Operator friendly

Cobots are designed for safe and efficient usage, making them easy to operate even for beginners. Additionally, they can be customized to suit different applications.

Durable

Built with industrial grade, durable materials, these cobots ensure lasting performance.
