Cobots: Reliable, Safe, Convenient

RAIV(Robot AI Vision)

Cobots are featured with Robot AI Vision that helps in the recognition and interaction with diverse objects.

Motion tracking and Recording

The cobots can respond to human actions via a controller. It also features a software that aids in the recording and mimicking of these human-guided movements.

2D Marker Positioning

The Cobots are equipped with a 2D robotic visual system that utilizes 2D cameras and visual markers that helps in adjusting the position of the robotic arm.

Safety Certifications

The Cobots meet the European safety standards and have obtained the cleanroom certification of highest standards. Thus, it ensures safe collaboration with human operators.

Operator friendly

The cobots are built to provide accurate output. Its functioning can be easily navigated by beginners. In addition to that it allows for custom software development features for expert level use.

Durability

Built sturdy with high-quality parts, a full aluminium frame, and gear systems, the Cobots ensure lasting performance.
